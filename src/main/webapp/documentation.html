USER GUIDE

Everything you need to know about using LOST&FOUND MUMBAI

How to Report a Lost Item

1

Click "Report Lost Item"

Start from the homepage by clicking the red "Report Lost Item" button.

2

Login or Register

If you're not logged in, you'll need to create an account first.

Password Requirements: At least 8 characters with 1 uppercase letter, 1 number, and 1 special character.
3

Select Category

Choose the most appropriate category for your lost item:

Product

Electronics, gadgets, clothing, etc.

Humans/Animals

Lost people or pets

Documents

IDs, passports, certificates

Valuables

Jewelry, watches, wallets

Vehicles

Cars, bikes, etc.

4

Fill Item Details

Provide all requested information including:

  • Item name and description
  • Location where it was lost
  • Date and approximate time
  • Distinguishing features
  • Upload photos (highly recommended)
5

Submit & Monitor

Your report will appear on the "View Lost Items" page where others can see it. You'll receive notifications if someone finds a matching item.

How to Report a Found Item

1

Click "Report Found Item"

Start from the homepage by clicking the "Report Found Item" button.

2

Follow Similar Steps

The process is similar to reporting a lost item - select category, provide details, and submit.

Important: If you found identification documents, please conceal sensitive information when uploading photos.

How to Claim a Found Item

1

Browse Found Items

Visit the "View Found Items" page and search by category or location.

2

Click "Claim This Item"

If you believe an item belongs to you, click the claim button.

3

Submit Claim Form

Provide proof of ownership and contact information.

4

Admin Verification

Our team will verify your claim within 48 hours. If approved, we'll connect you with the finder. You can check your approval status and the finder's contact information in the 'My Reports' section.

Safety Note: Always arrange meetings in public places for item exchanges.

Dashboard Features

1

View Lost Items

See all active lost item reports in a scrollable feed (like social media posts).

2

View Found Items

Searchable list of found items where you can claim matches.

3

My Reports

Track all items you've reported as lost or found, including claim status.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do items stay listed?

Active listings remain for 30 days. You can renew them if needed.

Is there a fee for using this service?

No, LOST&FOUND MUMBAI is completely free for all users.

What information should I avoid sharing?

Never share financial details, full ID numbers, or home addresses publicly.

How does the admin verification work?

Our team checks claim details against the found item description. We may request additional proof before connecting parties.

Can I report items found outside Mumbai?

Currently we only serve the Mumbai metropolitan area.
