Introduction

At Lost-and-Found Hub, your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, etc.
  • Item Information: Details of lost or found items you post.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, and other analytics data.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Facilitate connections between users posting lost and found items.
  • Improve the functionality of our platform.
  • Send updates and notifications about your posts or claims.

Data Protection

We implement robust security measures to protect your data. However, we cannot guarantee absolute security due to the inherent risks of internet communication.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data.
  • Opt-out of communications or notifications.
  • Contact us regarding privacy concerns.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our platform. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Third-party services may also use tracking technologies to provide analytics and improve our services.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted third parties only when necessary for service provision or when required by law.
